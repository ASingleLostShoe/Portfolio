Boulder Hazard-Risk Mitigation

ESRI Story Map November 2024

Overview

I created this StoryMap to provide a Hazard-Risk Mitigation Evaluation for the city of Boulder, CO. The StoryMap lays out a recent history of Boulder's experience with Natural Hazards, looks into future risks residents of the city should be aware of, and then provides government resources with information on existing mitigation programs.
